Beans ' Grinds

The type of bean and how you grind it will change the taste of your coffee more than anything else. This guide will help you choose beans you enjoy and grind them correctly for your brewing method.

Which Grind Size for Which Method?

Brewing Method Grind Size Why
French Press Coarse Prevents over-extraction
Pour Over Medium Balance extraction, clean taste
Espresso Fine Needed for pressure brewing
Mixing Extra Fine Dissolves into the drink
